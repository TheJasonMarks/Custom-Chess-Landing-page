Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Introduction

Custom Chess ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Custom Chess is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Local Storage: Game saves, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device only.
  • Custom Images: Any images you upload for custom pieces are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.
  • Purchase Information: If you make in-app purchases, payment processing is handled by Google Play or Apple App Store. We do not store your payment details.

Advertising

Our app may display advertisements. Ad providers may collect anonymous usage data to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

Data Storage

All your game data, custom pieces, and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it is not uploaded to any server.

Third-Party Services

Our app may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services (for Android)
  • Apple App Store (for iOS)
  • PayPal (for web store purchases)
  • Advertising networks

Children's Privacy

Custom Chess is rated 13+ due to optional Crude Humor content. The mature content is hidden by default and requires user action to enable. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You can:

  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the app
  • Clear app data through your device settings
  • Disable personalized ads through your device settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
