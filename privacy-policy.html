Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Introduction

Custom Chess respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Local Storage: Game saves and settings are stored locally on your device.
  • Custom Images: Images you upload are stored locally and not transmitted to servers.
  • Purchase Information: Payments are handled by Google Play or Apple App Store.

Advertising

Our app may display advertisements. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload your data to any server.

Children's Privacy

Custom Chess is rated 13+ due to optional Crude Humor content. We do not collect personal information from children under 13.
