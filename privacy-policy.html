Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Introduction

Custom Chess respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Local Storage: Game saves and settings are stored locally on your device.
  • Custom Images: Images you upload are stored locally and not transmitted to servers.
  • Purchase Information: Payments are handled by Google Play, Apple App Store, PayPal, or CashApp.

Advertising

Our free app may display advertisements. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload your data to any server.

Charitable Donations

100% of proceeds from the Diarrhea Word Set DLC are donated to The Hope Project via CashApp.

Children's Privacy

Custom Chess mobile apps are suitable for all ages. Optional mature content (Diarrhea Word Set) is only available on the website.
